Features

Everything you need to study effectively

Built with the realities of low-connectivity environments in mind, every feature works offline.

Task Management

Create, organize, and track tasks by subject and due date. Update status from Pending to In Progress to Completed with one click.

  • Subject-based organization
  • Due date tracking
  • Status workflow
  • Soft archive

Pomodoro Timer

25-minute focused study sessions linked to your tasks. Sessions are automatically saved and contribute to your study statistics.

Offline-First

All task operations work without internet. Data is saved locally using IndexedDB and automatically synced to the server when connection is restored.

Progress Analytics

Track weekly study hours, daily streaks, and your most-studied subjects. Visual charts give you insight into your learning patterns.

Secure Auth

JWT-based authentication with bcrypt password hashing. Your data is private, scoped to your account, and never shared.

Auto Sync

A smart sync queue processes all offline operations the moment your connection returns — no manual action needed.

Offline Technology

Built for Ethiopia's internet reality

We know connectivity is unreliable. EthioStudy was engineered from the ground up to be fully functional without internet access — not as an afterthought, but as a core requirement.

1
Internet disconnects

The app detects the connection change instantly and notifies you.

2
Keep working normally

Create, update, and archive tasks. Data is saved in IndexedDB on-device.

3
Connection returns

The sync queue processes automatically. Your data reaches the server — no duplicates, no data loss.
